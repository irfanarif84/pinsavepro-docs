# Pinterest Video Downloader Guide

PinSavePro provides a free Pinterest video downloader for public Pinterest video URLs.

Tool page: https://pinsavepro.com/video-downloader

## Steps

1. Copy the Pinterest video Pin URL.
2. Open PinSavePro.
3. Paste the URL.
4. Download the available video quality.

Use PinSavePro only for content you own, have permission to use, or are allowed to save for personal reference.
